Console_RPG

This was one of my longest projects. It is a Dungeons & Dragons-inspired console RPG where the game master is handled by a neural network.

I tried to make it as close to real DnD as I could, while also building a combat system in the style of Heroes of Might and Magic 3, including spells and turn-based battles.

The project does not work perfectly, and development has been abandoned, but it is still one of the biggest and most ambitious things I built.

Project scope

One of my longest builds

This project grew far beyond a simple prototype and turned into a long experiment around storytelling, combat systems, and AI-driven roleplay.

Core idea

Dungeons & Dragons with a neural GM

Instead of a human game master, the world flow and narrative control were pushed toward a neural-network-driven system.

Combat system

Heroes of Might and Magic 3 inspired

Battles were designed with a turn-based structure and spell usage that took heavy inspiration from Heroes of Might and Magic 3.

Current state

Ambitious but unfinished

The result is not perfect, and the development is no longer active, but the project still shows the scale of what I was trying to build.
