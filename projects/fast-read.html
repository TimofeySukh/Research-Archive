Fast_read

Fast_read is a bilingual controlled reading research app. It runs the same fixed 4-text study protocol in Russian and English and compares one-word-at-a-time playback against normal PDF page reading.

This is research I am actively running with the goal of eventually writing a study around a simple question: do we read faster and understand text better when we do not have to track the line and every word appears directly in front of us?

The repository is mainly used to run the study protocol and collect session data. You can take part in the research on onewordtext.tech.

Open research branch

Study design

Fixed 4-text protocol

The app runs the same structured reading study in both Russian and English so the results can be compared under the same controlled setup.

Reading modes

One word vs PDF page

The main comparison is between one-word-at-a-time playback and standard PDF page reading.

Repository role

Research branch first

The active branch is the research branch, not a polished product branch, because the current goal is data collection and protocol iteration.

Research question

Speed and comprehension

The project tests whether removing line-tracking effort can improve both reading speed and understanding.
